About Me

The story behind the code.

I'm Sudhiksha Sadige, an Honors Computer Science student at the University of Oklahoma, with a double minor in Data Analytics and MIS, plus an undergraduate certificate in Data Science.

My journey into tech started with curiosity: taking things apart, building small projects, and wondering how everything connects. That curiosity has grown into a passion that spans software engineering, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and data science.

I'm the kind of person who enters CTF competitions for fun, builds games in my spare time, leads an AI/ML club, and manages online communities with thousands of members. I believe technology is at its best when it's built by diverse teams solving real problems, and I want to be part of that.

Whether it's writing code, analyzing data, or leading a team, I approach every challenge with the same mindset: learn fast, build thoughtfully, and never stop asking questions.
